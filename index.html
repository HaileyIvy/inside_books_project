What We Do

IBP receives written requests from inmates for books and educational materials, we then select and ship books to them.
Learn More

Why We Do It

We believe that every prisoner has the right to quality reading and educational materials.
Learn More

How We Do It

We rely upon our volunteers and community to generously donate their time, books, and money towards our mission!
Learn More

History

IBP was founded in 1998 and has always been a community- focused and volunteer-run organization.
Learn More

Volunteer

Volunteers open letters from inmates, select books to best fit the request, and make packages of books to send back to each inmate. The books selected by the volunteers become the personal property of each inmate.

Donate

Inside Books Project has sent over a quarter million free books to inmates in Texas, funded entirely by donations from our community. Inside Books Project has no paid staff - all donations go directly to support our mission.
